Ethiopia boundary data in stanford-gy496hh6563-shapefile was downloaded from https://geodata.lib.berkeley.edu/catalog/stanford-gy496hh6563 on 7 November 2023.

Woreda shapefile in Ethiopia_AdminBoundaries-shp was downloaded from https://ethiopia.africageoportal.com/datasets/africageoportal::woredas-level-3/explore on 28 November 2023.  The woreda selected for kebele-level kriging and for generating the prediction surface is Dera in Amhara region.  One of the six kebeles (consisting of one cluster of points) in the woreda is dropped from analysis because its name was not reported.

The data_for_regs.csv file, which contains the survey data, is available upon request.
